Personality Quizzes

Knowing yourself is an important part of your journey as a trader. Take these quizzes to discover the trading style that suits you best, your preferred markets, and your comfort with risk. There are no wrong answers here — just honest ones.

01 Self-Assessment

Quiz: Which Trading Style Is Best For You?

Are you a scalper, day trader, swing trader, or position trader? (Revisit Module 20 for the full profiles.)

1. How much uninterrupted screen time can you realistically commit each day?

2. How do you feel about holding a position overnight?

3. How do frequent, fast decisions make you feel?

4. How important is it to you that trading costs (spread/commission) stay a small share of each trade?

5. What pace of life do you want trading to fit into?

02 Self-Assessment

Quiz: Which Currencies Should You Trade?

Finding a currency pair you're genuinely comfortable trading matters as much as your trading style.

1. What draws you to the markets most?

2. How do you feel about sharper, less predictable price swings?

3. Which type of analysis do you enjoy most (Modules 01–18)?

4. How do you want to hold positions, generally?

03 Self-Assessment

Quiz: What Is Your Attitude Towards Risk?

Each trader has their own risk comfort level — knowing yours shapes every rule in Module 24's risk management framework.

1. A trade you sized normally moves against you by your planned stop distance. How do you feel?

2. How would you describe your ideal risk per trade?

3. After three losing trades in a row, what's your instinct?

4. How do you feel about using leverage?
